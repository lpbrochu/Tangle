Keyboard & Interaction Guide

Graph Controls

  • Drag Background: Pan around the thought canvas.
  • Scroll Wheel: Zoom in/out to focus or see overview.
  • Drag Nodes: Move nodes dynamically to structure ideas manually.
  • Click Node: High-light the node and its connections; displays full statistics, snippet previews, and link mapping.
  • Click Background: Clear highlighted focus and hide the preview panel.

Markdown Architecture Syntax

  • Define Node: Write `# Node Name` on a new line.
  • Connect Node: Put `[[Target Node Name]]` anywhere in the body text under a header node.
  • Stub Nodes: Linking to nodes not defined yet creates a dotted "stub" node. Click it on the graph and click Create Note to initialize it instantly!
